What Veercal does

The honest car finance calculator

Built for Australian buyers who want to understand what they are actually committing to, not just the headline monthly payment.

📊
True total cost
Every dollar in, every dollar out. Finance costs, running costs, stamp duty, depreciation, and your exit value when you sell. One honest number.
⚖️
Side by side comparison
Compare cash, personal loan, dealer finance, finance lease, and novated lease using the same vehicle, same hold period, same running costs.
💼
Novated lease modelling
Full salary sacrifice and FBT calculation using ATO statutory rates. EV FBT exemption built in. Provider fee input. The most complete novated model available for free.
🚪
Exit simulator
What does it cost if you sell at year 2? Year 3? See your true position at any point in the ownership period, including cost per kilometre.
🏷️
On-costs included
Stamp duty calculated for all 8 states and territories. Luxury Car Tax modelled. EV government rebates factored in. Nothing hidden.
🔗
Save and share scenarios
Save your comparison and share it as a link. Open the same scenario on any device. Compare dealer quotes side by side before you walk in.
